Division 1 Team

The Division 1 team is the competitive team of the UOTC and consists of a Men’s and a Women’s team (15 men and 14 women). Student-athletes are presented with the exceptional opportunity to engage in tennis at a highly competitive level. We travel across Ontario, representing the Gee-Gees’ name, to play all the other six universities in the OUA league. Our season starts in early September and finishes in early October with the OUA championship. Our team is driven by unwavering motivation to achieve greatness. The top 8 men and women of this division are those who travel and compete, with the rest serving as reserves all fighting for spots on the active roster.


Practice Schedule

Division 1 players have morning practices every weekday during the competitive season from 7am to 9am at Rockliffe Lawn Tennis Club.
During the off-season, we hold practice up to 3 times a week (Days, time and location to be determined)

All of the men’s and women’s teams play together in order to grow as a together and raise each other up. Practices always start with a tennis specific dynamic and hitting warm-up followed by live ball and basket drills with aim to improve footwork and agility, finished with game situations and match play.


Tryout Information

September 1st from 6-9 pm and September 2nd from 1-4 pm at Rideau Sports Center.
Please contact us if you wish to try out for the team. Note that to participate in tryouts, there’s a non-refundable $25 fee to be paid.
