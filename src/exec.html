Meet the Team!




Ryan Zanganeh

Ryan Zanganeh is the President of the University of Ottawa Tennis Club. Since starting tennis at the age of six, he has been training, competing, and teaching kids & teens how to play tennis. Having dedicated over 14 years of his life to the sport, Ryan realizes the substantial positive physical and mental impacts playing tennis can have on a person’s life.

Ryan is currently a 3rd year student studying a Bachelor of Science in Human Kinetics with Honours at the University of Ottawa. He has done a TedTalk on Crohn’s Disease and is currently completing a post-operation rotator cuff protocol. Ryan is pursuing a career in orthopedic surgery with hopes of one day becoming a Medical Director on the ATP Tour.



Jacob Leong

Jacob has been training with some of the best tennis players in Ottawa since he was nine. So when he transitioned to university life at uOttawa it was only natural for him to join the UOTC. Taking up a leadership role in the club was his goal from the start and it landed him at the position of treasurer, as well as the coach for the UOTC’s division 2/3 teams.



Sara Zeineddine

Sara Zeineddine is the Communications Executive of the uOttawa tennis team. She is currently in her 3rd year of PoliSci. She is very invested in promoting the Tennis Club, especially on social media. Sara speaks four languages such as French, English, Spanish, Arab and is currently learning Russian as a new language, which is a great asset for the Communications position.

She has been playing tennis since a very young age. She’s always shared her love for tennis, to family, friends and even strangers. She competed as well both nationally and internationally. She also played and competed for several years in UP Club in Cornella de Llobregat and Academia Sanchez Casal in Barcelona, Spain.
