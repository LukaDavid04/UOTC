The University of Ottawa Tennis Club Executive Team is composed of four hard-working, and passionate uOttawa students dedicated to growing, improving, and promoting the University of Ottawa Tennis Club (UOTC). Each student on the Executive Team holds a respective role: President, Treasurer, Communications, Marketing, and Website Development. The respective student responsible for each role are shown below as well as a bio about who they are. For more information regarding the specific roles of the UOTC Executive Team, please refer to our club constitution.


Meet the Team!



Ryan Zanganeh

Ryan is the President of the University of Ottawa Tennis Club. Since starting tennis at the age of six, he has been training, competing, and teaching kids & teens how to play tennis. Having dedicated over 14 years of his life to the sport, Ryan realizes the substantial positive physical and mental impacts playing tennis can have on a person’s life.

Ryan is currently a 4th year student studying a Bachelor of Science in Human Kinetics with Honours at the University of Ottawa. He has done a TedTalk on Crohn’s Disease and is currently completing a post-operative rotator cuff recovery protocol. Ryan is pursuing a career in dentistry with interests in Pediatrics and Oral Surgery specialties.


Jacob Leong

Jacob is the Treasurer of the UOTC. He has been training with some of the best tennis players in Ottawa since he was nine. So when he transitioned to university life at uOttawa it was only natural for him to join the UOTC. Taking up a leadership role in the club was his goal from the start and it landed him at the position of treasurer, as well as the coach for the UOTC’s division 2/3 teams.

He is currently a 3rd year student studying an Honours Bachelors in Biomedical Sciences with a focus in Neuroscience. His belief in the positive, rewarding relationship between sports and the human psyche leads him to continue to be an ambassador of tennis, helping people of all ages discover a love for the sport and all the fulfillment it brings.


Sara Zeineddine

Sara is the Communications Executive of the uOttawa tennis team. She is currently in her 4th year of PoliSci. She is very invested in promoting the Tennis Club, especially on social media. Sara speaks four languages such as French, English, Spanish, Arab and is currently learning Russian as a new language, which is a great asset for the Communications position.

She has been playing tennis since a very young age. She’s always shared her love for tennis, to family, friends and even strangers. She competed as well both nationally and internationally. She also played and competed for several years in UP Club in Cornella de Llobregat and Academia Sanchez Casal in Barcelona, Spain.


Luka David

Luka is the Chief Technical Officer for the UOTC Exec Team. He is entering his third year in Software Engineering. Before coming to uOttawa, Luka competed mainly in the United States while going to school in Windsor, Ontario. He began coding in grade ten and after fast tracking a few computer science classes and participating in a few hack-a-thons, he decided to pursue his love of technology in university.

Luka is an avid tennis fan, passionate player and patient coach. He pitched the idea to make the website to the exec team, and has made a personal goal to help the team in all aspects, not just technical ones. His goal is to bring up the reputation of the university both on and off the court.
